Power Measurements

What is the difference between ERP and EIRP?
ERP = Effective Radiated Power (relative to half-wave dipole)
EIRP = Equivalent Isotropic Radiated Power (relative to isotropic antenna)

Conversion: EIRP = ERP × 1.64
Used in 600m band and some microwave bands
